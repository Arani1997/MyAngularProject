Old students Association

OSAs typically engage in activities such as organizing reunions, alumni networking events, fundraising campaigns for the school, and mentoring programs for current students. They may also provide scholarships or other forms of support to students in need. Being a part of an OSA can offer several benefits to alumni, such as networking opportunities, a sense of community, and a chance to give back to their school. Additionally, OSAs can help schools by increasing alumni engagement and support, which can be beneficial for recruitment, fundraising, and overall reputation.
